Frequently Asked Questions

What algorithm models calculate the alternative routes? ▾
Sky runs a multi-layered route optimization pass and stores each result with a deterministic hash so a saved trip can be verified later without re-running the calculation.
How does theme switching persist across page changes? ▾
Your theme choice is written to local storage and applied the moment a page loads, so it stays consistent no matter which page you land on.
Is ledger data logged server-side or locally? ▾
Every routing entry currently stays on your device in browser storage. Nothing is transmitted until you explicitly export it.
Can I use Sky.EcoAI without an account? ▾
Yes — the Basic tier and the route terminal work without signing in. Creating an account lets you save preferences and sync a profile across devices once that's enabled.
